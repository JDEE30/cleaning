How to clean a wood table

Gather your cleaning chemicals

For most wooden tables You can easily use water and soap on a cleaning rag.

Start wiping down your table

First, you will wipe down the top of the table. Secondly, you will wipe down the legs as well. Thirdly, you have any coffee stains or other stains on the wood table You can wipe your rag in circles. This can take a few minutes depending on how bad the stain is and how long the stain has been there.
